Robin Williams

The man who made the world laugh out loud

Here's a timeline of his life:

  • 1951 - Born in Chicago, Illinois.
  • 1967 - Moved to Marina County, California, with his family.
  • 1976 - Hired for the first time after an audition for a play.
  • 1980 - Interpreted Disney's Popeye.
  • 1991 - Between 1987-1991, Williams was an Oscar nominee three times: Good morning, Vietnam, Dead Poets Society and The Fisher King.
  • 1992 - Brought Aladdin's' Genie to life.
  • 1993 - Played one of his most recognized characters in Mrs. Doubtfire.
  • 1995 - Filmed successful Jumanji.
  • 1996 - Had major roles in hits such as Jack or The Birdcage.
  • 1997 - The movie Good Will Hunting won him the Oscar.
  • 1998-2013 - Had a successful career, playing characters in movies such as Flubber, Patch Adams, What dreams may come, Bicentennial man, A.I. Artificial Intelligence, Insomnia, One Hour Photo, Robbots, or Old Dogs.
  • 2014 - Died at age 63. The last movie he was in was Night at the Museum 3.

"I think the saddest people always try their hardest to make people happy. Because they know what it feels like to feel absolutely worthless and they don't want anybody else to feel like that." --Robin Williams
